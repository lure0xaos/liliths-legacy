Hello again, this is just a small hotfix to address two annoying bugs. The more serious of the two major bugs was occurring during sex, where a background error was being thrown and causing the game to softlock. The other bug which I felt needed fixing asap was where the inventory UI would be pushed down and cut off at the bottom of the screen if your enchantment instability attribute was over 99.

There are a few other bug fixes and very small changes in this version. I've also updated the rest of the tooltips to use the new formatting which clothing/items/weapons/tattoos have. Additionally, I've scaled down some of the larger character images, so the download size for this version is a lot smaller than it previously was. If you want the full resolution images, then you can get them from my github.

Thank you so much for your support, and for playing Lilith's Throne! I hope you enjoy the update!


Please note: You can transfer your saved games, exported characters, saved enchantments, transformation presets, and outfits from previous versions into new versions simply by copying the contents of your 'data' folder into the new version's 'data' folder!
